This section describes the specific methodological processes that transform an academic manuscript into a published and properly indexed article. Here we address the core tasks of editorial work: how to manage the flow from manuscript reception to publication, how to handle complex metadata of authors and institutional affiliations, and how to ensure that publications circulate effectively through indexers and academic databases.

The content of this level provides practical knowledge about editorial operations regardless of the specific tools used to execute them.
